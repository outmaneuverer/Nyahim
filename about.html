About Nyahim-Farm

Nyahim farm is dedicated at providing you with fresh, organic products; right from our farm to your table. We aim at providing our customers with not only affordable farm produce but also health reliable; e attain this by the use of almost free chemically processed materials in our farm. We make our own feeds in both fishfarming and poultry, and for our vegetables we prepare our manure from poultry wastes; which is more economical and ensures no wastes from our farm.
